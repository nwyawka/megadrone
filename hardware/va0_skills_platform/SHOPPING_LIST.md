## Flight Controller Options

Choose ONE flight controller:

| Option | Price | Pros | Cons | Link |
|--------|-------|------|------|------|
| **Matek F405-Wing** | $45-55 | Built-in OSD, BEC, current sensor, fixed-wing focused | Requires soldering | [Matek Systems](https://www.mateksys.com/?portfolio=f405-wing) |
| **SpeedyBee F405 Wing** | $45-52 | App configuration, easy setup | Often out of stock | GetFPV, RaceDayQuads |
| **Pixhawk Mini** | $80-100 | Full ArduPilot support, expandable | Larger, heavier | Holybro, ReadyMadeRC |
| **Kakute F7 Mini** | $40-50 | Compact, reliable | Quad-focused but works | GetFPV, RaceDayQuads |

**Recommendation:** Matek F405-Wing - best value for fixed-wing
